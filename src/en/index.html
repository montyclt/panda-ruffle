What happened?

On Wednesday, March 26th, Panda suffered a digestive arrest and was admitted to the NIDO veterinary clinic, in Valencia. After spending three days in intensive care, she passed away on Saturday the 29th.

The autopsy revealed a condition compatible with VHD/RHD (viral hemorrhagic disease). Initially, it was believed to be the cause (I made it public in this post), but the PCR test was negative for both types. In the end, the exact cause of her death could not be determined with certainty. I have attached the veterinary case report.

Raffle Details

The Prize

I'm raffling a PLOOHT pet water fountain, valued at approximately 45 euros. I chose this model because it was the one Panda used to drink water, and I wanted something that could be useful for any type of pet, encouraging more people to participate.

Goal and Use of Funds

The total cost of Panda's hospitalization was 589.56 euros. Additionally, there were extra expenses such as cremation, transportation, and preventive tests for my other pets. This raffle aims to help recover most of this expense.

If more than 550 euros are raised, all the excess will be donated entirely to the MINIPOW rabbit shelter in Valencia, Spain.

Participation and Prize Delivery Conditions

The raffle is international. Anyone with a valid PayPal account can participate.

  • If you win, and you're in Spain: You can choose between receiving the water fountain directly from Amazon or receiving 45 euros via PayPal.
  • If you win, and you're outside Spain: The prize will be 45 euros via PayPal transfer.

Winner Announcement

The winner will be announced on January 1, 2026, on my Twitter/X account (@montyclt) and also on the Instagram account (@pelusa.panda).

Why Now?

Although several months have passed, I'm organizing this raffle now because I needed time to be able to organize it with a clearer mind.
